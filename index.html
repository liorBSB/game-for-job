The AI Upsell Simulator

A Quick Note: This PWA demo was built entirely using AI (Cursor, Claude Code, Gemini). It was incredibly fun seeing an interactive concept come to life in under 90 minutes!

1. The Idea

Upselling is a Muscle: Traditional training is often long and ineffective. We use short, engaging micro-learning to turn upselling into an instinctive reflex.

Mental Product Mapping: By practicing focused scenarios, employees automatically learn to link specific customer baskets directly to the right complementary products.

2. The Experience

Self-Paced Autonomy: Employees use the app for just 5–10 minutes daily on their own terms (like during a commute), replacing mandatory training sessions.

Gamified Incentives: Engagement is driven by competition—points, branch leaderboards, and “Top Seller” bonuses—rather than forced participation.

So, Why Does This Work?

3. The Impact

Safe-Space Practice: Pitching to an AI removes the fear of customer rejection. After 20 AI pitches, doing it on the real floor feels completely natural.

Rewiring Behavior: Upselling fails because it feels awkward. Bite-sized daily repetition rewires this, turning a forced effort into a seamless habit.

4. What’s Next? (Roadmap)

Live POS Integration: The AI will pull real store data to train employees on pitching actual overstock or active promotions.

Hyper-Personalization: The system learns each employee’s weak spots (e.g., struggling to upsell premium items) and tailors scenarios specifically to them.
